## Austin Live Data

Austin is a city of **real-time information**. Lake levels, water temperatures, traffic conditions, power grid status, air quality — these data points affect daily decisions for millions of Central Texans. Our live data hub brings it all together in one place.

## Water & Lake Conditions

**Lake Travis** and **Lake Austin** water levels fluctuate significantly with rainfall and drought cycles. Our trackers pull real-time data from LCRA sensors, showing current elevation, historical trends, and boat ramp accessibility. Water temperature readings across multiple locations help you plan swimming and water activities.

## Traffic & Infrastructure

Austin's traffic — especially on **I-35, MoPac, and 183** — can make or break your commute. Our traffic map shows real-time congestion, construction zones, and incident reports. During severe weather, we also track power outage maps from Austin Energy.

## Air Quality & River Flow

Real-time **AQI monitoring** from EPA stations across the metro, plus Colorado River flow rates from USGS gauges. Essential data for outdoor planning, especially during wildfire smoke season and high ozone summer days.
