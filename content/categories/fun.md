## Fun in Austin

Austin doesn't take itself too seriously — and that's what makes it **one of the most fun cities in America**. Whether you're planning a date night, entertaining kids on a Saturday, or just looking for something delightfully weird to do on a Tuesday afternoon, Austin delivers.
